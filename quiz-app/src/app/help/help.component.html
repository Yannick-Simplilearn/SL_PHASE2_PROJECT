GENERAL INFORMATION

Please read this information before starting the quiz. It contains the rules regarding the attendece of the quiz as well as some helpful advice!

  1. Rules of attendence

    While taking the quiz, the attendees are not allowed to use any tools which provide advantages over other attendees such as browsing the Internet, using phones etc.. Any violation will lead to an exclusion from the quiz.

  2. Contents of the quiz

    The quiz is designed for testing applicants in the knowledge of 3 different programming languages: [1]Powershell, [2]JavaScript, [3]C. The applicant needs to answer 5 questions for each language.

  3. Evaluation

    Each correct answer is rewarded with 1 point whereas a wrong answer (or a not given answer) will count with a value of zero. (There are no negative points!)

  4. Further questions

    For any further questions, please ask your supervisor. However there will be no help for answering the questions, nor will you get hints or tipps. Stick to asking questions that regarding facing technical problems.

BEST OF LUCK AND SUCCESS! ~ YOUR QUIZ-APP TEAM!
